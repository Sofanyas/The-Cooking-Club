Chocolate Milk: Cold milk, Chocolate syrup
Directions: Pour cold milk into a glass and add chocolate syrup to the milk. Stir well until the chocolate syrup is fully dissolved. Enjoy your delicious chocolate milk!
Scrambled Eggs: Eggs, Butter, Salt, Pepper
Directions: Beat eggs. Melt butter in a pan. Pour eggs into the pan. Stir until cooked. Season with salt and pepper. Serve and enjoy!
Turkey Bacon: Turkey bacon slices
Directions: Preheat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Place turkey bacon slices in the pan. Cook until crispy on both sides. Remove from the pan and drain excess grease on paper towels. Serve and enjoy your turkey bacon!
